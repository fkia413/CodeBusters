Meet Code Busters (formerly known as Churro Busters)

Roberto, Corvus, Farhana and Farah...the dream team. We're a rowdy bunch who one day were tasked to create a website for QA Cinemas. Our origin story was in, fact, making churros...


We took our products to Disneyland, where we put up a big sign asking people if they thought we should give up making churros to start writing code for websites. Underneath the sign, we had a QR code that took people to a website where they could vote 'yes' or 'no. At the end of the weekend, we tallied up the votes and it turned out that 100% of the votes were for 'yes', so we resigned from our jobs the next day and got cracking.


A few hours after being job-free, we found out that the 'no' button was, in fact, broken.


A month later, we had a website that displayed our skills, enough use cases to fill a medium-sized swimming pool with, and zero tolerance for the bloke who created our initial voting poll.
